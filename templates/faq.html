    FAQ

    How do I register on ipsrjobs.com?

    Click on register now link, it will take you to application form. Fill out all details, upload your resume and submit.

    Does it cost anything to register?

    Registration is absolutely free, no need to pay anything at any stage of selection

    What is Ipsr registration number?

    Ipsr registration number is the unique registration number for students who have undergone training from Ipsr. For non Ipsr students leave space as blank.

    Do I need to upload my photo?

    Photo is not mandatory. 90% profile completeness is enough

    Do I need to upload my resume?

    Resume is mandatory. When you apply for a job, your resume will be going to the company. If you donâ€™t upload resume your application will not be received at the company.

    What if I forget my user name or password?

    You can click on Forget password link to reset your password

    Do I need to complete my profile?

    Yes, you need to complete your profile more than 85%

    Do I need a valid email id for registration?

    Yes, further communication from companies might be through emails. So you need an email id to register your profile.

    How can I update/edit my Profile?

    Login to your profile and go to profile settings, there you can update your profile details

    Who all have access to my Resume?

    Only Ipsrjobs administrator has the access to your profile and resume.

    How do I obtain a list of job openings?

    You can either check latest vacancies or you can search any job category in the search box

    How do I know if this job is still available?

    You can apply only for active jobs. If the job is not available, then apply now button will be displaying expired.

    How do I find out the status of my application?

    Your application will be directly going to companies, if you get shortlisted companies will directly contact you for further rounds.

    What happens when I submit my application?

    Your application will be directly going to companies, if you get shortlisted companies will directly contact you for further rounds.

    How will the department contact me?

    HR from the companies will take your contact details from your resume and directly call you. Make sure contact details in your resume is up to date to get interview calls.

    What are job alerts?

    We will send jobs matching your profile to your email id

    How do I create an effective job alert?

    You can subscribe our newsletter by submitting your email id at Newsletter window at right bottom corner (Footer) of Ipsrjobs home page

    How do I stop job alerts?

    You can unsubscribe your email id from our newsletter database.

    Why do I get jobs that aren't exact matches?

    You might have chosen categories wrongly.

    I am not getting enough jobs in my inbox. What should I do?

    Subscribe Newsletter by submitting your email id at Subscribe Newsletter window at the right bottom footer. What is the help line number of ipsrjobs? 9446922478. Call us between 9:00 AM and 5:00 PM for urgent matters only.

    How much does it cost to post a job?

    Job posting is absolutely free.

    How do I post a job?

    You can directly submit your details at Post Vacancies link at home page. (http://www.ipsrjobs.com/employer_zones/post_vacancy)

    How long does a job post remain on the site?

    Job posting will be active for a period of minimum one week, can be extended based on request.

    Is there any limitations for number of postings?

    No you can post any number of vacancies.
